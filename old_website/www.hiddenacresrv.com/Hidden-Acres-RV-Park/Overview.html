OVERVIEW

Welcome to Hidden Acres RV Park
in Princeton, Texas !

"Texas Saver Card - 15% discount available"

High Speed Wireless Internet Access

A quiet and friendly place to relax. Located on Lake Lavon just 15 miles east of Plano. Close to boat ramps and swimming areas. South Fork Ranch is only a 10 mile drive. Downtown Dallas is a short 30 minute drive. You are only 15 minutes to major mall, movie complex and restaurants. Gas and propane are nearby. No freeway - no railroad - no street lights. Clear view of the stars at night.

McKinney - East: Hwy 380 (9 mile) to Princeton, south on FM 982, (9 mile) to RD 437, or
McKinney - South: /
Plano - North: Hwy 75 to exit 33 (Bethany), east (7 mile) to Lucas 'Food Mart', east on 3286 (5 mile) to FM 982, south on 982 (2 mile) to RD 437,
